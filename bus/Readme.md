
### To Puebla from Terrapuerto El Ángel

Yes, you can take a direct executive-class bus to Puebla from the Terrapuerto El Ángel (located at Río Tiber 98, near Paseo de la Reforma). These buses, often operated by e-bus or Pullman Travel, provide a convenient, comfortable service from this central Mexico City location directly to the Paseo Destino terminal in Puebla.

Key Details:
- Location: Terrapuerto El Ángel, Río Tiber 98, near the Angel of Independence.
- Service: Executive-class buses (e-bus/Pullman).
- Destination: Generally arrives at Puebla Terminal Paseo Destino.
- Amenities: Waiting room, coffee, and luggage lockers are available.
- Alternative: Estrella Roja operates similar frequent services from the Mexico City Airport (MEX) to Puebla.
