


Aeromexico flies to over 45 destinations within Mexico, utilizing their main hub at Mexico City International (MEX Terminal 2) and major bases in Guadalajara (GDL) and Monterrey (MTY). Other key served cities include Tijuana, Cancún, Mérida, Puerto Vallarta, Los Cabos, and Felipe Ángeles International (NLU).

### Main Hubs:

Mexico City (MEX), Guadalajara (GDL), Monterrey (MTY).
